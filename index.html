About

The person behind INCONNU BOY

My name is Dawens, but most people online know me as INCONNU BOY. I live in Maringá, in the south of Brazil, and I've been writing code seriously since 2023. I didn't come from a computer science background — I taught myself by opening a code editor, breaking things, and figuring out why they broke, night after night, until it started making sense.

My first real project was a WhatsApp automation bot, built because I wanted a tool that simply didn't exist yet. That project turned into more projects, and eventually into the thing I do almost every day now. My repositories are used by thousands of people across different countries, and my main project has been forked over 1,200 times by other developers building on top of what I made — that number still means a lot to me every time I see it.

I'm not interested in overselling myself. What I can promise is this: if you hire me, you're not getting a middleman or an agency — you're getting the developer who actually writes and tests every line, replies to your messages personally, and stays reachable after the project is delivered. That's the standard I hold myself to.
